Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about implementation, security, and ROI.

How quickly can we see results after implementation?
Most clients see immediate improvements in processing speed within 48 hours of deployment. Full ROI typically realizes within 30-60 days as teams adapt to the automated workflow.
What types of documents can your AI process?
Our AI handles all common insurance document formats including PDFs, images, emails, handwritten forms, and structured data files. The system learns and adapts to your specific document types.
How does pricing work for teams of different sizes?
Our platform starts at $5,000/month for unlimited processing. This typically covers teams of 3-10 underwriters. Larger enterprise deployments have custom pricing based on volume and feature requirements.
What security measures protect our sensitive data?
We maintain SOC 2 Type II compliance, use bank-grade encryption, and provide complete audit trails. All data is processed in secure, isolated environments with no data retention unless specified.
Do you integrate with existing insurance management systems?
Yes, we offer pre-built integrations with major insurance platforms and a flexible API for custom integrations. Our team handles the technical setup to ensure seamless data flow.

  • Communication Records: Records of our communications with you, including emails and demo sessions

We use the information we collect for the following business purposes:

  • Respond to Inquiries: To contact you about demo requests and answer your questions about our AI automation platform
  • Provide Services: To deliver demos, consultations, and information about our insurance automation solutions
  • Business Communications: To send you relevant information about our services, industry insights, and product updates
  • Improve Our Services: To analyze website usage and improve our platform and customer experience
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and protect our business interests

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate our business (e.g., email services, analytics providers)

We never share your information with competitors or use it for purposes unrelated to our insurance automation services.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:
